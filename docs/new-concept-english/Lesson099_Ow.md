## 自学导读

### get up

站起来。这一短语还可表示"起床"。

### be afraid that / be sure that

想，恐怕 / 想，认为。这两个结构后面通常接从句，在口语中有时 `that` 可省略。

## 语法 Grammar in use

### 宾语从句（1）

宾语从句在句中作宾语，一般用 `that` 引导，但在口语中经常省略掉 `that`。

可用在 `say`, `think`, `believe`, `hope`, `know`, `understand`, `suppose` 等动词之后：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>She says that she is cold.</summary>
<p>她说她冷。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He thinks that he needs an X-ray.</summary>
<p>他认为他需要拍个 X 片。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I know that I can repair this car.</summary>
<p>我知道我能修理这部车。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>They believe that they will have more money.</summary>
<p>他们认为他们会有更多的钱。</p>
</details>

可用在某些描写感情的形容词之后，如 `afraid`, `sure`, `sorry`, `glad` 等：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I am sure that you need an X-ray.</summary>
<p>我觉得你需要拍个 X 片。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I am sorry that you are ill.</summary>
<p>听到你生病了我很难过。</p>
</details>

### 间接引述时的时态

如果间接引语中的引述动词是现在时，那么其后的时态通常与原来口头陈述句的时态相同。转述刚刚说完的话时通常皆如此。

## 词汇学习 Word study

### slip v.

（1）滑倒：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>She slipped and fell down on the wet stones.</summary>
<p>她脚下滑了一下，跌倒在潮湿的石头上。</p>
</details>

（2）滑落；脱落：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The soap slipped out of her hand.</summary>
<p>肥皂从她的手中滑落。</p>
</details>

（3）下滑；下跌：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Profits continue to slip this year.</summary>
<p>利润今年呈持续下跌趋势。</p>
</details>

（4）遗忘；忽略：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Our wedding anniversary completely slipped my mind.</summary>
<p>我把我们的结婚纪念日忘得一干二净。</p>
</details>

### hurt v.

（1）弄痛；使受伤：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Have you hurt yourself?</summary>
<p>你伤着自己了吗？</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He fell off the bicycle and hurt his leg.</summary>
<p>他从自行车上摔下来，摔伤了一条腿。</p>
</details>

（2）危害；损害：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>That case has hurt his reputation.</summary>
<p>那个案件损害了他的声誉。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I don't mean to hurt you.</summary>
<p>我本无意伤害你的感情。</p>
</details>

### help v.

（1）帮助；援助；救助：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He devoted his life to helping the disabled.</summary>
<p>他付出一生的心血去帮助那些残疾人。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Would you help me to lift this box?</summary>
<p>你能否帮我抬一下这只箱子？</p>
</details>

（2）对……有帮助；对……有好处：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>My knowledge of French helped me a lot while I was travelling in France.</summary>
<p>我在法国旅行时，我的法文知识帮了我大忙。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Humour can help in a tense situation.</summary>
<p>幽默能够缓和紧张局面。</p>
</details>

| be sure that | 确信 |
| be sorry that | 遗憾 |
| be glad that | 高兴 |

这些结构后面通常接从句，口语中 `that` 可省略。

### 宾语从句

宾语从句在句中作宾语，一般用 `that` 引导（口语中常省略）。

可用于以下动词之后：

> say, think, believe, hope, know, understand, suppose

也可用于以下形容词之后：

> afraid, sure, sorry, glad
