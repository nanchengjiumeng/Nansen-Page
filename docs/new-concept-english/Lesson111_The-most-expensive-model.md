## 自学导读

### buy on instalments

以分期付款的方式购买。

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I have bought this house on instalments.</summary>
<p>我用分期付款的方式买了这所房子。</p>
</details>

### and then fourteen pounds a month for three years

……然后每月 14 镑，3 年付清。`then` 和 `fourteen` 之间省略了 `pay`。

## 语法 Grammar in use

### 形容词的平级比较级：as…as

形容词的比较形式有 3 种：较高比较级（`more expensive`, `the most expensive`）；较低比较级（`less expensive`, `the least expensive`）；平级比较级（`as expensive as`）。

请看下面句中 `as…as` 的用法：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The green apple is as sweet as the red one.</summary>
<p>这个绿苹果同那个红苹果一样甜。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The policeman is as tall as the policewoman.</summary>
<p>这个男警察与那个女警察个子一样高。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The white knife is as sharp as the black one.</summary>
<p>这把白色的小刀与那把黑色的小刀一样锋利。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I don't know as many people as you do.</summary>
<p>我认识的人没有你认识的多。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Robert is not as old as he looks.</summary>
<p>罗伯特没有他看上去那么老。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The blue car is not as clean as the red car.</summary>
<p>蓝车没有红车干净。</p>
</details>

## 练习答案 Key to written exercises

### Lesson 112

#### A

1. This book is cheap, but that one is cheaper.
2. This book is expensive, but that one is more expensive.
3. This question is easy, but that one is easier.
4. This question is difficult, but that one is more difficult.

#### B

1. Is the policeman as tall as the policewoman? No, he isn't. The policeman isn't as tall as the policewoman.
2. Is the man as short as the woman? No, he isn't. The man isn't as short as the woman.
3. Is the boy as old as the girl? No, he isn't. The boy isn't as old as the girl.
4. Is the red pencil as blunt as the green pencil? No, it isn't. The red pencil isn't as blunt as the green pencil.
5. Is the blue car as clean as the red car? No, it isn't. The blue car isn't as clean as the red car.
6. Is the woman as fat as the man? No, she isn't. The woman isn't as fat as the man.

#### C

1. No, it isn't. It is more expensive. It's the most expensive radio I've ever seen.
2. No, he isn't. He is more intelligent. He's the most intelligent person I've ever met.
3. No, it isn't. It is more interesting. It's the most interesting book I've ever read.
4. No, it isn't. It is less difficult. It's the least difficult test I've ever done.

## 词汇学习 Word study

### afford v.

（1）买得起；付得起（钱）：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>She can't afford a new coat.</summary>
<p>她买不起一件新大衣。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>We are not rich enough to afford a car.</summary>
<p>我们并不太富有，不能购置汽车。</p>
</details>

（2）担负得起（时间等）：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I feel I can't afford any more time on this project.</summary>
<p>我觉得我不能再在这个项目上花更多的时间。</p>
</details>

### deposit

（1）n. 预付定金；押金；保证金：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He put down a deposit on the house yesterday.</summary>
<p>昨天他付了房子的定金。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>You'll have to pay a deposit of $60 to reserve the room.</summary>
<p>要预定这个房间，你需交付 60 美金的押金。</p>
</details>

（2）v. 存放；存储：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>We were advised to deposit our valuables in the bank safe.</summary>
<p>别人建议我们将自己的贵重物品存放在银行的保险箱里。</p>
</details>
