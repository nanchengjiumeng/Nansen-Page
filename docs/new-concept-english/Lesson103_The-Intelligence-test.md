## 语法 Grammar in use

### too 与 enough

#### (1) enough 的用法

A. 在形容词之后使用：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He failed the exam because it wasn't easy enough.</summary>
<p>他考试不及格，因为题不够容易。</p>
</details>

（不说 `enough easy`）

B. 在名词之前使用：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He didn't buy the car because he didn't have enough money.</summary>
<p>他没买那部车，因为他没有足够的钱。</p>
</details>

C. 可以用在 `enough…for sb./sth.` 和 `enough…to do sth.` 结构之中：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>She hasn't got enough money for a holiday.</summary>
<p>她没有足够的钱去度假。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>This dress isn't big enough for her.</summary>
<p>这件衣服对她来说不够大。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>She's not old enough to live alone.</summary>
<p>她未到独自生活的年纪。</p>
</details>

#### (2) too 的用法

A. `too` 表示"过于"：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I can't go out. It's too hot.</summary>
<p>我无法外出，太热了。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>She couldn't answer the questions because they were too difficult for her.</summary>
<p>她无法回答问题，因为对她来说太难了。</p>
</details>

B. 可以用在 `too…for sb./sth.` 结构之中：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>This skirt is too big for me.</summary>
<p>这条裙子对我来说太大了。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>It's too easy for me.</summary>
<p>这对我来说太容易了。</p>
</details>

C. 可以用在 `too…to do sth.` 结构之中：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The exam was too difficult for him to pass.</summary>
<p>考试太难了，以至于他无法通过。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The box is too heavy for you to carry.</summary>
<p>这个盒子太沉了，你没法扛起。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>She is too young to live alone.</summary>
<p>她年纪太小了，无法独自生活。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>It's too far to walk home from here.</summary>
<p>从这儿步行回家太远了。</p>
</details>

请比较：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The wall is too high for them to climb over.</summary>
<p>这堵墙太高了，他们无法攀越过去。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The wall is low enough for them to climb over.</summary>
<p>这堵墙矮到如此之程度，以至于他们可以攀越过去。</p>
</details>

## 词汇学习 Word study

### fail v.

（1）失败；不及格：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Doctors failed to save the old man's life.</summary>
<p>医生们未能挽救那位老人的生命。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He failed his French paper because it was too difficult.</summary>
<p>他的法语考试没及格，因为试卷太难了。</p>
</details>

（2）（身体等）衰退；变弱；凋谢：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>My eyesight is failing.</summary>
<p>我的视力在衰退。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The flowers failed for lack of sunshine.</summary>
<p>花因缺少阳光而凋谢。</p>
</details>

（3）（后接不定式）不，不能；忘记：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I fail to see why you find it so extraordinary.</summary>
<p>我不明白为什么你们认为它如此与众不同。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He failed to persuade me.</summary>
<p>他没能说服我。</p>
</details>

### hate v.

（1）讨厌；不喜欢；有反感：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He hates exams!</summary>
<p>他讨厌考试！</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I hate beef.</summary>
<p>我不喜欢牛肉。</p>
</details>

（2）憎恨；憎恶：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He said that he hated hypocrisy.</summary>
<p>他说他憎恶虚伪。</p>
</details>

### too / enough 对比

| 结构 | 用法 | 例句 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| too + 形容词 | 太…… | too difficult |
| too + 形容词 + for sb. | 对某人来说太…… | too difficult for me |
| too + 形容词 + to do | 太……以至于不能 | too difficult to pass |
| 形容词 + enough | 足够…… | easy enough |
| enough + 名词 | 足够的…… | enough money |
