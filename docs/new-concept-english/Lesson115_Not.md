## 语法 Grammar in use

### 不定代词

（1）`every`（每一），`no`（无），`any`（若干）及 `some`（一些）可与 `one`，`thing` 构成复合代词，与 `where` 构成复合副词。不定代词可作主语、宾语和表语。

#### A. 作主语

这些代词作主语时都作单数看待，因此使用单数谓语动词。

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Everyone is asleep.</summary>
<p>大家都睡了。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Everything is untidy.</summary>
<p>一切都乱糟糟的。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Nobody has told me about it.</summary>
<p>没有人告诉过我有关这件事的情况。</p>
</details>

#### B. 作宾语

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I've got nothing to wear.</summary>
<p>我没有穿的衣服了。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I've heard nothing.</summary>
<p>我没听到什么动静。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I saw no one.</summary>
<p>我谁也没有看到。</p>
</details>

#### C. 作表语

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>There's nothing on the shelf.</summary>
<p>架子上什么也没有。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>There's somebody in the garden.</summary>
<p>花园里有个人。</p>
</details>

（2）与 `some` 与 `any` 的用法一样，`somebody/someone/something/somewhere` 一般用于肯定句；`anybody/anyone/anything/anywhere` 用于疑问句与否定句；`nothing/nobody/no one/nowhere` 相当于 `not anything/anybody/anyone/anywhere`：

</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I don't know anyone here.</summary>
<p>这儿我谁也不认识。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">

<p>这个架子上有什么东西吗？</p>

## 课堂笔记

### 不定代词总结

| 词 | 用法 |
| --- | --- |
| someone/somebody/something | 用于肯定句 |
| anyone/anybody/anything | 用于疑问句和否定句 |
| no one/nobody/nothing/none | 表示"没有" |
| everyone/everybody/everything | 表示"每个" |

### 作主语时谓语动词用单数

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Everyone is asleep.</summary>
<p>大家都睡了。</p>
</details>

### nothing / anything / everything

| 词 | 含义 |
| --- | --- |
| nothing | 什么也没有 |
| anything | 任何东西（用于疑问/否定） |
| everything | 一切事情 |

### None / Nothing at all

`at all` 用于加强否定语气：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Nothing at all.</summary>
<p>什么也看不见。</p>
</details>

### lemonade 相关

> a glass of lemonade 一杯柠檬水
> some lemonade 一些柠檬水
