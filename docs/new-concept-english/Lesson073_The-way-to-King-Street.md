## 自学导读

### She does not know London very well

她对伦敦不很熟悉。`know…well` 这一短语意为"对……了解"。又如：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I don't know him very well.</summary>
<p>我不太了解他。</p>
</details>

### and she lost her way

……因此她迷路了。句中的 `and` 当"所以"讲，表示结果。`lose one's way`，迷路。

### ask (sb.) the way

（向某人）问路。

### say to oneself

心中暗想。注意：`talk to oneself` 意为"自言自语地说"。

### Can you tell me the way to King Street, please?

您能告诉我到国王街怎么走吗？`tell sb. the way (to)`，告诉某人（去……的）路。

## 语法 Grammar in use

### 副词

副词（adverb）这个词的本意是补充动词的意义。这就是许多副词的作用。它们可以通过修饰动词告诉我们有关句中某个动作的情况，也就是告诉我们某事是如何、何时、何地等发生或进行的。

副词可以是单个的词（如 `slowly`）或词组（如 `very well`）。单一副词既有以 `-ly` 结尾的也有不以 `-ly` 结尾的（如 `quickly`, `fast`）。

#### 形容词向副词的转换

（1）在形容词后面直接加 `-ly`，如：

> quick—quickly
> hurried—hurriedly
> pleasant—pleasantly
> warm—warmly

（2）以 `-y` 结尾的形容词，则把 `-y` 改成 `-i`，再加 `-ly`，如：

> thirsty—thirstily
> happy—happily

（3）形容词与副词形式相同：

> late—late
> fast—fast
> hard—hard
> well—well

## 词汇学习 Word study

### lose v.

（1）迷失；（使）迷路：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>She did not know London very well, and she lost her way.</summary>
<p>她对伦敦不很熟悉，因此迷了路。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>It's very easy to lose your way in a strange city.</summary>
<p>在一个陌生的城市里，你很容易迷路。</p>
</details>

（2）失去；丧失：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He lost his sight in a car accident.</summary>
<p>他在一起汽车交通事故中失明了。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>She has just lost her job because of carelessness.</summary>
<p>她刚刚因疏忽大意而丢了工作。</p>
</details>

（3）遗失；丢失：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I can't enter my house because I've lost my key on my way home.</summary>
<p>我进不了自己的房子，因为在回家的路上我把钥匙丢了。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>We lost her in the crowd.</summary>
<p>我们在人群中找不见她了。</p>
</details>

### understand v.

（1）理解；懂：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He doesn't understand English and you can try French.</summary>
<p>他不懂英语，你可以试试法语。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I don't understand what you mean.</summary>
<p>我不明白你的意思。</p>
</details>

（2）明了；了解；得知：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>How the machine works is still not fully understood.</summary>
