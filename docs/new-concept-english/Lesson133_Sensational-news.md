## 自学导读

### Have you just made a new film, Miss Marsh?

您刚拍完一部电影吗，马什小姐？`make` 可表示"作出某种举动"，其意义根据宾语而定。本句中的 `make` 可理解为"拍（摄）"。

### make another

再拍一部。之后省略了 `film`。

### by our reporter

= `written by our reporter`，由本报记者撰写。

## 语法 Grammar in use

### 需改变时态的间接陈述句

间接引语里常要改变时态，这是因为原来讲的话与转述这些话之间有一段间隔。在间接引语里通常并不确切地重复讲话者说过的话。转述通常发生在过去，所以引述动词通常是过去时。因此，转述时从句的时态通常要"往回移"（back shift）。一条普遍的规则是：现在时变为过去时，过去时变为过去完成时。

#### （1）一般现在时改为一般过去时

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>'I'm thirsty,' she said.</summary>
<p>She said (that) she was thirsty. 她说她渴了。</p>
</details>

#### （2）现在进行时改为过去进行时

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>'I'm having a rest,' she told them.</summary>
<p>She told them (that) she was having a rest. 她告诉他们说她当时正在休息。</p>
</details>

#### （3）现在完成时改成过去完成时

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>'I've just made a new film,' she told me.</summary>
<p>She told me (that) she had just made a new film. 她告诉我说她刚完成一部新影片。</p>
</details>

#### （4）一般将来时改成过去将来时

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>'I'll have to ask my husband,' she said.</summary>
<p>She said (that) she would have to ask her husband. 她说她将得去问问她丈夫。</p>
</details>

#### （5）情态动词由现在时改成过去时或条件时态

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>'I can see you tomorrow,' she said.</summary>
<p>She said (that) she could see me the next day. 她说她第二天可以见我。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>'I may return at six o'clock,' she told me.</summary>
<p>She told me (that) she might return at six o'clock. 她告诉我说她可能 6 点钟回来。</p>
</details>

#### （6）为过去时或条件时态的情态助动词不变

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>'I could see you tomorrow,' she said.</summary>
<p>She said (that) she could see me the next day. 她说她第二天会见我。</p>
</details>

## 词汇学习 Word study

### sensational adj.

（1）令人兴奋的；轰动性的：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The discovery was sensational.</summary>
<p>那个发现引起了轰动。</p>
</details>

（2）〈贬〉耸人听闻的；企图追求轰动效应的：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The sensational news report didn't have much effect on them.</summary>
<p>那起耸人听闻的新闻报道对他们并无多大影响。</p>
</details>

（3）〈口〉非常好；给人深刻印象的：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>She looks sensational in that dress!</summary>
<p>她穿那条裙子的样子真令人难忘！</p>
</details>
