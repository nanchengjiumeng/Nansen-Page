<span class="nce-lesson-line">TOM: What are you doing, Peggy?</span>

<span class="nce-lesson-line">TOM: What do we need?</span>
<span class="nce-lesson-line">PEGGY: We need a lot of things this week.</span>

## 语法 Grammar in use

### must 与 need

#### (1) must

`must` 表示"必须"、"应当"：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I must go to the grocer's.</summary>
<p>我得去趟杂货店。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>You mustn't make a noise!</summary>
<p>你不该弄出噪音来！</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Must I call the doctor?</summary>
<p>我必须请大夫吗？</p>
</details>

#### (2) need

`need` 表示"需要"、"必须"。作助动词时多用于疑问句和否定句，如：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Need I make an appointment?</summary>
<p>我需要约一下时间吗？</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>You need not hurry.</summary>
<p>你不必太匆忙。</p>
</details>

`need` 也可作实义动词，这时就要有人称、数及时态上的变化，疑问句中也需用助动词 `do`。如：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>We need a lot of things this week.</summary>
<p>我们这周需要许多东西。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He needs some money.</summary>
<p>他需要一些钱。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Do you need any sugar?</summary>
<p>你需要一些糖吗？</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>What do they need this week?</summary>
<p>他们这周需要什么东西？</p>
</details>

## 词汇学习 Word study

### hope

（1）v. 希望；盼望；期待：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I hope that you'll have a lovely vacation.</summary>
<p>我希望你能有一个愉快的假期。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Mark's hoping to study Law at Harvard.</summary>
<p>马克盼望着进入哈佛大学学习法律。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I hope that you've got some money.</summary>
<p>我希望你有了些钱。</p>
</details>

（2）n. 希望，期望；指望：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>We are full of hope for the future.</summary>
<p>我们对未来充满信心。</p>
</details>

（3）n. 期望着的事；被寄予希望的人：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>His hope is that his son will get married and settle down soon.</summary>
<p>他所希望的是他儿子能早点结婚，安顿下来。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He is a young man of genius, the hope of Russian poetry.</summary>
<p>他是一位年轻的天才，是俄罗斯诗歌的希望所在。</p>
</details>

### need

（1）v. 需要：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>We need some honey.</summary>
<p>我们需要些蜂蜜。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Do you need any help?</summary>
<p>你需要帮忙吗？</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Does he need to know?</summary>
<p>他需要知道吗？</p>
</details>

（2）n. 需要（物）；必要：

<details class="nce-translation">

<p>不必担心。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">

### much / many 的用法

| 限定词 | 修饰 | 例句 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| much | 不可数名词 | We haven't got much tea. |
| many | 可数名词复数 | We haven't got many tomatoes. |

| 类别 | 物品 |
