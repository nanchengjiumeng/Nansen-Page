## 自学导读

### make up our minds

打定主意。`make up one's mind` 是表示下定决心的一种固定用法。

### Will you travel by sea or by air?

你们乘船去，还是乘飞机去？这是一个选择疑问句。`by sea` 表示"乘船"。`by air` 表示"乘飞机"。

### take a long time

花很长时间。

## 语法 Grammar in use

### 情态助动词 may 表示可能性

`may` 或 `might` 都表示"可能""也许"。通常既可以用 `may`，也可以用 `might`，不过 `might` 所表示的可能性要比 `may` 小，且当情况是虚拟的时只能用 `might` 而不能用 `may`。

#### （1）may 表示现在或将来的可能性：may + 动词原形

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>The bread may be fresh.</summary>
<p>面包可能是新鲜的。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He may be reading.</summary>
<p>他可能正在看书。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I may go abroad.</summary>
<p>我可能出国。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>They may offer me a job.</summary>
<p>他们也许会给我提供一份工作。</p>
</details>

#### （2）may 也可表示过去的可能性，过去式为：may have (done)

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>He was late. He may have been busy.</summary>
<p>他迟到了。他可能一直很忙。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>I went out last night, and she may have been reading.</summary>
<p>我昨天晚上出去了，她可能一直在看书。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>It may have been right.</summary>
<p>也可能是对的。</p>
</details>

## 词汇学习 Word study

### worry v.

（1）担忧；发愁：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Mum always worries too much and it makes her tired.</summary>
<p>妈妈总是过度忧虑，而这使她疲惫。</p>
</details>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>She's worried that she might lose her job soon.</summary>
<p>她担心也许很快她就会丢掉她现在的工作。</p>
</details>

（2）困扰；使不安宁：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Doesn't it worry you that his health is becoming worse?</summary>
<p>他的身体越来越差，你不担心吗？</p>
</details>

### look after

（1）照料，照顾，照管：

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Who's going to look after the garden when we are away?</summary>
<p>我们离开时谁来照料花园呢？</p>

<details class="nce-translation">
<summary>Sally will help us to look after the children tomorrow.</summary>
<p>萨莉明天会帮我们照看孩子们的。</p>
</details>

（2）注意；关心：

<details class="nce-translation">

## 课堂笔记

### may 表示可能性

| 结构 | 含义 |
| --- | --- |
| may + 动词原形 | 现在/将来可能 |
| may be doing | 现在可能正在做 |
| may have done | 过去可能做了 |
| may have been doing | 过去可能一直在做 |

### make up one's mind

下定决心，打定主意。

> I can't make up my mind. 我拿不定主意。

### by sea / by air / by land

| 短语 | 含义 |
| --- | --- |
| by sea | 乘船 |
| by air | 乘飞机 |
